Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is WAYMATES?

WAYMATES is a platform to connect travelers . You can find ride shares, post your ride offers, and save on travel costs.

How do I post a ride?

To post a ride, simply click on the 'Post a Ride' section, fill in your details including your origin, destination, date, time, and available seats, and submit.

How do I search for rides?

To search for a ride, go to the 'Find a Ride' section, enter your origin, destination, and travel date, and click 'Search Rides'.

How do I contact the person offering the ride?

You can contact the ride offerer using the mobile number listed in the ride details.

Is it safe to share a ride with strangers?

Safety is a priority for us. We recommend checking the ride details, reading the ratings of the drivers (if available), and communicating with the ride offerer before making a decision.
