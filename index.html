Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is WAYMATES?

WAYMATES is a platform to connect travelers. You can find ride shares, post your ride offers, and save on travel costs.

How do I post a ride?

Go to the 'Post a Ride' section, fill in your ride details and submit.

How do I search for rides?

Go to the 'Find a Ride' section, enter your route and date, and click 'Search Rides'.

How do I contact the person offering the ride?

The mobile number will be shown in the ride details after search.

Is it safe to share a ride with strangers?

We recommend verifying ride details, checking ratings if available, and having a phone conversation before deciding.
